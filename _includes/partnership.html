Stripe Atlas and Merchise

On March 18, 2016 Stripe announced the expansion of Stripe Atlas to include Cuban entrepreneurs. Stripe Atlas initially launched on February 24, 2016. During the beta period, entrepreneurs will need an invitation from Stripe or one of their partners around the world, including Merchise Startup Circle in Cuba.
